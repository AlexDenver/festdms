Abhyuday

Abhyuday is an annual inter-collegiate technical fest organized by the Department of Master of Computer Applications at Ramaiah Institute Of Technology, which serves as an excellent platform for students for various colleges to display their talents and ideas.

Abhyuday 5.0, conducted last year was a grand success. There were participants from various colleges all over Bangalore.

This year we bring back Abhyuday 6.0, the sixth edition of the fest. With a string of 11 competitions, and exciting cash prices to be won, Abhyuday 6.0 is going to be two days of absolute fun!
